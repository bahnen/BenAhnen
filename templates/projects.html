How Do Iowans Purchase Liquor - 2020

In this project, my team and I created an analysis of how, when, and where Iowans buy liquor.

Situation: Iowa liquor sales from the beginning of 2019 until 2021.

Data: This project will use data that is publicly available from the data.iowa.gov website. We downloaded data from the specified time period and loaded it into R. The CSV files contain data pertaining to the date the liquor was sold, regional information on the sale, quantity sold, and total sales amount in dollars from January 2019 until 2021.

Question: Our group would like to explore and answer, “Did the pandemic cause liquor sales to increase or decrease?”

Hypothesis: We suspect that there will be a large increase in the sale of liquor prior to the initial closing of businesses due to public hysteria. However, once the public realizes that the pandemic is here to stay, we expect there to be increased liquor sales overall

St. Paul to Minneapolis Traffic Analysis - 2022

In this project, I analyzed west bound traffic data between St. Paul and Minneappolis, looking what weather patterns contributed to the amount of traffic.

Situation: St. Paul to Minneapolis west bound traffic from 2012 until 2018.

Data: This project will use data that is publicly available from the UCI Machine repository website. I downloaded data from the specified time period and loaded it into R. The CSV files contain data pertaining to the date the traffic was captured.

Question: I wanted to explore and answer, “Does certain weather types or time of the year cause traffic levels to increase or decrease?”

Hypothesis: I suspect that weather types will have some impact on the average traffic, however I believe clear skies will have the highest average because more people will want to be outside and traveling.

Supply Chain Crisis: Reddit Analysis - 2022

In this project, my team and I analyzed reddit threads pertaining to the supply chain crisis, looking to see how the public perceived the lasting impacts of the crisis.

Situation: Reddit posts pertaining to the supply chain crisis from November 2021.

Data: This project will use data that is webscraped from reddit. We downloaded data from the specified time period and loaded it into R. The CSV files contain data pertaining to the date the reddit threads were posted.

Question: we wanted to explore and answer, “How are reddit users feeling about the supply chain crisis?”

Hypothesis: We suspect that individuals will largely have a negative outlook due to the holiday season coming up and people worrying about ordering/receiving their gifts on time.
