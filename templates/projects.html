NFL Playoff Simulation - 2020

In this project, my team and I created an NFL playoff simulation using python. We were inspired by the lack of sports due to the global pandemic to create a way for users to satiate their sports needs.

We first used the beatiful soup package to scrape NFL rankings from online into a dictionary. Then weights were assigned to each team based on their rank and a formula was created to use these weights in our simulation. Next, we used nested loops to pit each selected team against each other in a single elimination playoff.

When running the simulation,the user is prompted with the option to select any 16 teams from the NFL. Once all teams are selected the simulation runs and gives updates on what teams advanced through each round until a winner is decided.

Iowa City Traffic Fatality Analysis -2019

In this project, I used government data on vehicle accidents in the state of Iowa to identify key conditions that resulted in fatal or serious accidents in Iowa City.

I first began by identifying key factors and then cleaned the data so that it best suited my needs. I then normalized my data in Excel and created pivot tables and bar plots to better visualize my data. Next, I conducted tests of independece to find the correlation between the conditions of the accident and the resulting injuries.

Conclusions:

There are a higher amount of crashes occuring in the summer months when school is out of session and these crashes have a higher probability of resulting in serious injury.

Accidents on snowy roads are much less likely to result in injury, but accidents on wet roads are more likely to result in injury.

Drinking and driving is most likely to result in a car crash injury during the early morning hours of the day
